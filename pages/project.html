Chicago has the most 'green roof' area of any US city, following an intiative by the EPA to combat the urban heat island effect while improving air quality. The first building to participate in the project was the City Hall, where it succeeded in reducing roof temperatures by over 35 degrees, while saving around $5000 USD in energy costs. It was soon followed by many other major buldings, including Millenium Park and the Soldier Field football stadium. The city now has over 500 green rooftops, covering more than 7 million sq ft of roof area.
